Elva Lopez started Elvas Cleaning over 15 years ago with a simple but powerful motivation — to create new opportunities for herself and her family. What began as a personal venture quickly grew into a trusted name in the community, built entirely on word of mouth and the quality of the work.

From the very first client, Elva set a standard that still defines the company today: show up on time, do the job right, and treat every space as if it were your own. That personal commitment is what turned first-time customers into long-term clients.

Today, Elvas Cleaning operates with a dedicated team that shares those same values. Whether we're cleaning a family home on a weekly schedule or maintaining a commercial office space, we bring the same level of care and attention to every job.

We serve both residential and commercial clients, and we take pride in being flexible, responsive, and easy to work with. No job is too small and no space is too big — we're here to help.
